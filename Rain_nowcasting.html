Rain nowcasting with Radar reflectivity maps

    Supervisor: Dominique Béréziat
    Contact: dominique.bereziat@lip6.fr

    Description:

    Rainfall forecasting is an important environmental issue (flood anticipation, agricultural management, …). Here we are interested in a short-term forecast (time horizon up to 2 hours). We use Radar data which are robust rainfall indicators. The algorithm will be based on deep learning approaches with an up-to-date architecture.
    • review literature. In particular, investigate the field of video prediction, and also relevant metrics, such as Structural Similarity,
    • choose one or two models, implement, train, and study the performances
